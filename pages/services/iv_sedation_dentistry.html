I.V. (Intravenous) Conscious Sedation Dentistry

I.V. Conscious Sedation is a combination of medicines to help you relax (a sedative) and to block pain (an anesthetic) during a dental procedure. You may stay awake but may not be able to speak, and you may not remember the procedure. In our office a certified nurse anesthetist will administer and monitor your sedation throughout the entire procedure.
