Anthony DeLucia, D.D.S.

Anthony DeLucia, D.D.S. is a 1983 graduate of Rollins College in Winter Park, Florida where earned a Bachelor's Degree in Biology and Chemistry. He received his Doctorate of Dental Surgery Degree from Georgetown University Dental School in Washington D.C. in 1987.
Dr. DeLucia has maintained a private practice in Stuart since 1987, focusing on cosmetic and reconstructive dentistry. He has been voted Best Dentist by Hometown news 2013 and 2014 Readers' Choice.
